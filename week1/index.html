Kema Noodles Recipe

Ingredients Steps

Ingredients

  • Noodles - 200g
  • Minced meat - 100g
  • Onion - 1, chopped
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Soy sauce - 2 tbsp
  • Vegetables - as desired

Steps

  1. Boil noodles and set aside.
  2. In a pan, saut√© onion and garlic.
  3. Add minced meat and cook thoroughly.
  4. Add vegetables and soy sauce, stir well.
  5. Mix in the boiled noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes.
